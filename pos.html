⏰ Happy Hour / Time-Based Discounts

Schedule automatic discounts for specific time windows. Active schedules apply automatically when the cashier processes items.

How it works: When a Happy Hour schedule is active, the product grid shows a ⚡ banner. At checkout, the cashier sees the automatic discount applied. Discounts can apply to all products or a specific category.
📡 Barcode Scanner / Reader

USB or Bluetooth barcode scanners work automatically — they send keystrokes like a keyboard. No configuration needed.

The scanner input field is always active in the POS screen. Just scan and the product adds to cart instantly.

📱 Camera / Mobile Scanner

Use your phone or computer camera to scan barcodes and QR codes. Click the camera icon in the POS scan bar.

  • Click 📷 button in POS scan strip
  • Point camera at barcode
  • Auto-detects in <1 second
  • Works with QR codes and most 1D barcodes
  • Flashlight toggle available on mobile
🗄 Cash Drawer Settings

Cash drawers connect via receipt printer (RJ11/RJ12 port) or USB. The drawer opens when a kick command (ESC/POS pulse) is sent through the printer.

Note: Browser-based drawer control is limited. For full hardware integration, use the POS with a local print server or Electron wrapper.
